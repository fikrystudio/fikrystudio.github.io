Crash OR crown

I'm Youssef Fikry, a brand identity designer and textile engineering researcher based in Egypt. My journey has always been driven by a simple feeling: a responsibility to make a real difference. Whether I'm in a design studio or a research lab, that purpose remains the same.

I believe a strong brand should do more than look good; it should work. It should provide clarity, structure, and confidence for a business to show up as its true self.

That’s why my design process is shaped by the same principles that drive my academic work: deep learning, smart questions, and a methodical approach to finding the truth. I don't guess; I study your market, your audience, and your competitors to uncover what makes you different. I then use this blend of strategic design and academic rigor to help you build an identity with purpose, ensuring the final result is not just visual, but meaningful and built to scale.
